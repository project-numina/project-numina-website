Our Mission

Open Data

We curate and publish high-quality mathematical datasets, making them freely available to researchers and developers worldwide.

AI for Formal Reasoning

We develop advanced AI models capable of understanding, solving, and formalizing complex mathematical problems with precision.

Human-AI Collaboration

We create intuitive tools and platforms that enable seamless collaboration between human mathematicians and artificial intelligence.

Numina is a non-profit organization. The donations and potential prize money from AI/Math competitions are exclusively used to support the development of the project.

AGI
Founding Team

There is something very special with mathematics.

Mathematics is a domain accessible to everyone, even to children long before they can read. Some of the greatest mathematicians of all time were actually self-taught, like Srinivasa Ramanujan, born to a modest family in India in 1887.
Mathematics is essential to humanity—it's the backbone upon which we've built everything from commerce to iPhones and nuclear power plants. Yet solving maths problems can often feel like playing a game with the universe.
This is why, when we started Numina, going open-source and open-dataset was the natural option. As with human intelligence, we believe that progress in AI for maths should be universal.
If the computer is a bicycle for the mind, then artificial intelligence is its engine—opening new horizons for the Ramanujans of our time.
"Mathematics transcends intelligence like an endless ocean only the mind can sail."
